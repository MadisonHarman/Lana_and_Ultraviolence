Summary of Key Findings

Lana Del Rey’s melancholic yet calm voice when singing these songs provides a stark contrast to her lyrics, which challenges her many listeners to think deeper about her intentions behind these songs. Both the critique and romanticization of her past relationships provides a uniquely real perspective on the issue; Lana is not trying to tell others that this reality is “good” or “correct,” but aims to bring awareness on the grueling and drawn-out nature of misplaced love due to cultural norms. With her fame, Lana is able to reach a wider audience, making her clear growth as a person throughout the years even more impactful, no matter if one agrees with her as an artist or not.

Lana's Current Stance On Relationships

At Variety's eighth-annual Hitmakers event this past weekend, Lana expressed her appreciation towards her recent marriage in a world where not everyone may see her as a moral person.
